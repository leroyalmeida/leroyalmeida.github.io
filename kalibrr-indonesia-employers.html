Kalibrr Indonesia Employers

Worked with the Indonesia group to create a new landing page to cater for their growing number of clients in the country.

The challenge was to create a high-converting employer sign-up page to maximize their growth.

User interviews indicated that employers value three things:

  • a strong database of candidates,
  • online assessments, and
  • a feature-full applicant tracking system.

Furthermore, we added supporting statements from current well-known client partners to emphasize these three value propositions.
