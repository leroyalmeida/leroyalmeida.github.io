Title: So Good They Can't Ignore You: Why Skills Trump Passion in the Quest for Work You Love
Author: Cal Newport
Year Read: 2019
Buy This Book: Amazon
Summary: Apply a craftsman’s mindset of focusing on becoming really good at what you do. Deliberate practice and constant feedback are key to quickly becoming really good.
